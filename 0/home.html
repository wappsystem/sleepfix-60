Welcome to the SleepFix60 Study.

An online research study to improve your
sleep and daytime functioning

You have been invited to take part in an online research study using a
new smartphone application that aims to improve your sleep and
daytime functioning.

SleeFix is a smartphone application designed to treat insomnia using
sleep consolidation therapy. Sleep consolidation therapy works to
improve the balance between actual sleep time and the total amount of
time spent in bed.

This study is for individuals who have sleep problems, are over the age
of 60 and have access to a smartphone.

To determine if you are eligible to participate, you will be asked to
complete a short series of questionnaires (max. 5-10 minutes).
you will know right away if you are eligble for the study.

You participation in this study is voluntary
